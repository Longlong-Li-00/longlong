About

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at Chonnam National University. My work centers on multimodal sensing of in vitro cardiac tissue and drug cardiotoxicity screening, and I welcome collaboration, project discussion, and academic exchange.

Research

Current topics include:

Integrated 3D bioelectronic scaffolds and tissue engineering
Developing 3D electrically stimulating scaffolds with embedded flexible sensors to build highly biomimetic human ischemic cardiac models.
Micro/nano sensor hardware and MEMS fabrication
Designing and validating flexible multimodal sensors based on MEMS processes for electromechanical monitoring.
In vitro cardiotoxicity screening and maturation platforms
Building stimulation platforms that promote cardiac tissue maturation and support high-throughput drug screening.
