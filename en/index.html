About Me

I am currently a PhD student at Chonnam National University. My research focuses on multimodal sensing for in vitro myocardium and drug toxicity screening. I am open to academic collaborations and discussions. [cite: 10]

Research Directions

Integrated 3D Bioelectronic Scaffolds
Developing 3D scaffolds with integrated flexible sensors for real-time monitoring of human ischemic heart models. [cite: 12]
Micro-nano Sensors & MEMS Fabrication
Design and validation of flexible multimodal sensors based on MEMS manufacturing processes. [cite: 13]
In Vitro Cardiotoxicity Screening Platform
Developing stimulation platforms to promote cardiomyocyte maturation for high-throughput drug screening. [cite: 14]
